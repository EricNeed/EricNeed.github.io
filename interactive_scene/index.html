Submarine Game

game where you drive a submarine and go to a location deep in a underground tunnel, avoid hitting on the rock and get there sucessfully, inspired by barotrsuma

  • move left: A
  • move right: D
  • climb: W
  • use station: E, LMB(click station)
  • esc station: R, LMB(click station)
  • editor mode: M

tip: to use a station, make sure your sprite is overlaping with the station and press E or click on the block
